Learn About us and Our Comapny!



Natural Beauty is a proudly South African, women-founded skincare brand created by Iqra Jassiem and Amahle Sani. What began as a small store in Cape Town has grown into a thriving company with a new headquarters and flagship store. Our clean, cruelty-free, and vegan products are made with natural ingredients to promote healthier skin and a better lifestyle. Our mission is to make skincare affordable while caring for the environment through recyclable packaging. With a vision to become South Africa’s most well-known women-run skincare brand, we’re excited to expand with new stores in Johannesburg and Pretoria. Designed for young adults up to their mid-thirties, our skincare is loved by both men and women who want effective, natural, and ethical beauty solutions.
