Benefits for Skin

Jojoba oil

Benefits for Skin

  • ☆Jojoba oil is lightweight and similar to the skin's natural oils. It balances sebum production, moisturises without blocking pores, and is suitable for sensitive or acne-prone skin.

Benefits for Skin
